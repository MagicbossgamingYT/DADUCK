How to Play

Welcome to Da Duck Chess! Here are the basic rules:

  • Each player starts with 16 pieces: 1 king, 1 queen, 2 rooks, 2 knights, 2 bishops, and 8 pawns.
  • The goal is to checkmate your opponent's king, putting it in a position where it cannot escape capture.
  • Players take turns moving one piece at a time.
  • Each type of piece has its own movement rules.
  • For detailed rules, consider looking up chess guides online.
